Volunteer State: Is Tennessee Vols vs. Alabama football still a rivalry?
Tennessee's victory over Auburn on Saturday changes the outlook for the rest of the Vols' season. But does it offer hope of a competitive game this week when the Vols (3-3, 1-2 SEC) host No. 1 Alabama (7-0, 4-0)?
John Adams and Blake Toppmeyer of the Knoxville News Sentinel join the Tennessean's Joe Rexrode to recap Tennessee's first SEC victory since 2016 and preview the Alabama game.Also, the crew discusses whether the "Third Saturday in October" remains a rivalry, given that Alabama has won 11 straight in the series.
